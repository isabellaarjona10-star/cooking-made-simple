Healthy High-Protein Banana Oat Pancakes

Why they're great: These pancakes are affordable, filling, and packed with protein using simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup oats (blended into oat flour)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Add eggs, oat flour, baking powder, and peanut butter.
  3. Mix thoroughly until batter is combined.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease.
  5. Pour small amounts of batter and cook 2-3 minutes per side.

Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

Why it's great: This is a budget-friendly, no-cook breakfast high in protein and perfect for busy mornings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup frozen or fresh berries
  • 1/4 cup oats or granola
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Spoon half the yogurt into a bowl or cup.
  2. Add a layer of berries.
  3. Sprinkle oats or granola on top.
  4. Repeat layers and drizzle with honey.
