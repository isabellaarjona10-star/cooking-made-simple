Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup berries
  • Granola
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Layer yogurt in a glass.
  2. Add berries and granola.
  3. Drizzle honey and serve.

Banana Oat Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup oats
  • ¼ cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mash bananas and mix with oats.
  3. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Bake 12-15 minutes.
