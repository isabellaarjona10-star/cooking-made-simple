Oven Baked Salmon with Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup asparagus

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Season salmon with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bake 12-15 minutes until fully cooked.
  4. Cook rice according to package directions.
  5. Steam asparagus and serve together.

Ground Turkey Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • Taco seasoning
  • Whole wheat tortillas
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground turkey over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and stir.
  3. Warm tortillas.
  4. Assemble tacos with toppings.
