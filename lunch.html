High-Protein Garlic Herb Chicken & Rice Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup uncooked white or brown rice
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Rinse 1 cup of rice under cold water. Cook according to package directions (about 15-20 minutes for white rice or 40-45 minutes for brown rice).
  2. While rice cooks, pat the chicken breast dry with a paper towel.
  3. In a small bowl, mix garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Rub the seasoning evenly over both sides of the chicken.
  5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. Cook chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  7. Steam broccoli for 5-6 minutes until tender.
  8. Slice the cooked chicken and assemble your bowl with rice, broccoli, and chicken.

Healthy Tuna Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat tortilla
  • 1 can tuna (in water)
  • ½ avocado
  • Spinach or lettuce
  • Light mayo or Greek yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Drain tuna and mix with yogurt or light mayo.
  2. Spread mashed avocado on tortilla.
  3. Add tuna and greens.
  4. Wrap tightly and slice.
